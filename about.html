About Eigensound

eigensound|lab is an open-source research and education project dedicated to exploring the intersection of mathematics, theoretical physics, and digital audio. Our primary focus is on "sonification" — the process of translating data and mathematical models into sound — and its alternative visualization techniques.

We research and develop novel sound synthesis techniques based on the principles of quantum mechanics and general relativity. By using concepts like eigenmodes, eigenvalues, and wave functions, we generate complex sound structures and timbres.

This website serves as a central preview hub for interactive web applications focused on sonification and visualization, research papers, and demonstration videos.
