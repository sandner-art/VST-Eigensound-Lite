About

Touch Synthesizer v2.0

Interactive musical instrument with motion control, multi-touch synthesis, and real-time effects processing.

Touch Controls:

• Touch & hold: Play sustained notes

• Quick tap: Trigger percussion beats

• Two-finger pad mode: Note + effect control

• Multi-touch: Create harmonies

Motion Controls:

• Shake device: Cycle through instrument modes

• Tilt device: Modulate sound parameters

Modes:

• SYNTH: Classic multi-touch synthesis

• DRUMS: Rhythmic patterns and beats

• FX: Sound effects and processing

• AMBIENT: Atmospheric soundscapes

• PAD: Two-finger collaborative playing

PAD MODE: First finger plays notes • Second finger controls effects
